TZ=GMT date -d "1970/01/01 00:29:36" +%s

Convert HH:MM:SS into seconds
date -ud "1970/01/01 00:29:36" +%s

uses the -u switch for UTC

Another way could be

echo $(($(date -ud "00:29:36" +%s)%86400))
