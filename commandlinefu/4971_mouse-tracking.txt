Mouse Tracking

Will track your mouse and save it to a file.

You can use gnuplot to graph it:

gnuplot -persist <(echo "unset key;unset border;unset yzeroaxis;unset
xtics;unset ytics;unset ztics;plot './mouse-tracking' with points lt 1 pt
6 ps variable")
