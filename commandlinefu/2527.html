kynnjo
$ cp -bfS.bak filename filename

could be shortened to

$ cp -bfS.bak filename{,}

...and if you don't mind the default backup suffix (~), it could be even shorter:

$ cp -bf filename{,}

Personally, when doing backups, I prefer to throw in the -a flag, so:

$ cp -abf filename{,}
