Substrings a variable

var='123456789'; echo ${var:<start_pos>:<offset>} 
~$ var='123456789'; echo ${var:4}
56789
~$ var='123456789'; echo ${var:4:2}
56

substrings a variable starting at position.

If no offset given prints rest of the line

Very useful. You can further cut the substring down without going all the way to the end. A third value allows you to specify the length of the desired substring.

var='123456789'; echo ${var:4:2}

56

Works in bash & zsh

Does not work in /bin/sh nor /bin/dash
