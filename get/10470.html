Create iso image of cd/dvd

dd if=/dev/cdrom of=~/cdimage.iso
Create iso image of cd/dvd

Alternatives

dd if=/dev/cdrom of=~/cdrom_image.iso
Rip a CD/DVD to ISO format.

An easy method to generate ISOs from CD/DVD media.
