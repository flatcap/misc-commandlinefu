Find broken symlinks

Locate broken symlinks in the current directory. Also useful, to remove broken links:

find . -type l ! -exec test -e {} \; -print0 | xargs -0 rm

What others think

There is also a utility called "symlinks" on some Linux systems which is much easier to type out.

'symlinks -r . | grep dangling' would be the same as the first use.

'symlinks -rd .' will recursively delete dangling links.
