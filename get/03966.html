Execute most recent command containing search string.

!?<string>? 
# !?sdb1?
cryptsetup luksOpen /dev/sdb1 fs_backup
Enter luks passphrase:

You can add :p at the end to prevent execution and just show you the command.
Example:
# !?sdb1?:p
cryptsetup luksOpen /dev/sdb1 fs_backup
