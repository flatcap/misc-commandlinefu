Copy a file structure without files

find * -type d -exec mkdir /where/you/wantem/\{\} \;

i used to use something like this until i needed to preserve ownership & permissions... enter cpio pass-copy mode:

find * -type d | cpio -pd /where/you/wantem

...better & faster than find -exec
