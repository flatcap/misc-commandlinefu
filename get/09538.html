Convert seconds into minutes and seconds

echo 'obase=60;299' | bc 
 04 59
Convert seconds into minutes and seconds

This is a very simple way to input a large number of seconds and get a more useful value in minutes and seconds.

Alternatives

bc <<< 'obase=60;299' 
 04 59
Convert seconds into minutes and seconds

This is a very simple way to input a large number of seconds and get a more useful value in minutes and seconds. Avoids useless use of echo.
